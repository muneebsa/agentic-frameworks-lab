Flagship Smartphone Comparison 2024

Camera Comparison:
- iPhone 15 Pro: 48MP main, 12MP ultra-wide, 12MP telephoto (3x optical zoom)
- Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra: 200MP main, 12MP ultra-wide, dual telephoto (3x and 5x optical zoom)
- Google Pixel 8 Pro: 50MP main, 48MP ultra-wide, 48MP telephoto (5x optical zoom)

Winner: Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra for versatility and megapixel count

Display Comparison:
- iPhone 15 Pro: 6.1-inch, 2556x1179, 120Hz ProMotion, 460 ppi
- Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra: 6.8-inch, 3120x1440, 1-120Hz adaptive, 2600 nits peak
- Google Pixel 8 Pro: 6.7-inch, 2992x1344, 1-120Hz adaptive, 2400 nits peak

Winner: Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra for size, resolution, and brightness

Battery Life:
- iPhone 15 Pro: Up to 23 hours video playback, fast charging to 50% in 30 minutes
- Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra: 5000mAh, 45W fast charging, wireless charging
- Google Pixel 8 Pro: 5050mAh, 30W wired, 23W wireless charging

Winner: Tie between Samsung and Google for battery capacity

AI Features:
- iPhone 15 Pro: Focus on computational photography and Photonic Engine
- Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra: Galaxy AI with on-device processing, translation features
- Google Pixel 8 Pro: Most advanced AI with Magic Eraser, Best Take, Audio Magic Eraser, Live Translate

Winner: Google Pixel 8 Pro for AI innovation

Software Updates:
- iPhone 15 Pro: Typically 5-6 years of iOS updates
- Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra: 7 years of OS and security updates
- Google Pixel 8 Pro: 7 years of OS and security updates

Winner: Tie between Samsung and Google

Price Comparison:
- iPhone 15 Pro: Starting at $999
- Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra: Starting at $1,199
- Google Pixel 8 Pro: Starting at $999

Winner: Tie between iPhone and Pixel for value

Overall Recommendation:
- Best for photography enthusiasts: Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra
- Best for AI features: Google Pixel 8 Pro
- Best for ecosystem integration: iPhone 15 Pro
- Best value: Google Pixel 8 Pro or iPhone 15 Pro
