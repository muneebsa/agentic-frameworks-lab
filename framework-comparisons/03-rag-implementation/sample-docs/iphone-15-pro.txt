iPhone 15 Pro - Technical Specifications

Display:
- 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display
- ProMotion technology with adaptive refresh rates up to 120Hz
- Always-On display
- Resolution: 2556 x 1179 pixels at 460 ppi

Chip:
- A17 Pro chip
- 6-core CPU with 2 performance and 4 efficiency cores
- 6-core GPU
- 16-core Neural Engine

Camera System:
- Pro camera system (48MP Main, 12MP Ultra Wide, 12MP Telephoto)
- 3x optical zoom in, 2x optical zoom out; 6x optical zoom range
- Digital zoom up to 15x
- Photonic Engine for improved low-light performance
- Night mode, Deep Fusion, Smart HDR 5
- ProRAW and ProRes video recording

Battery Life:
- Up to 23 hours video playback
- Up to 20 hours streaming video playback
- Fast-charge capable: Up to 50% charge in 30 minutes with 20W adapter or higher

Storage Options:
- 128GB
- 256GB
- 512GB
- 1TB

Connectivity:
- 5G capable
- Wi-Fi 6E (802.11ax)
- Bluetooth 5.3
- USB-C connector with USB 3 speeds up to 10Gb/s

Water Resistance:
- Rated IP68 (maximum depth of 6 meters up to 30 minutes)

Operating System:
- iOS 17

Price:
- Starting at $999
