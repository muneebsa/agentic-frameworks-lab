# Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra Technical Specifications

## Overview
The Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra is Samsung's premium flagship smartphone featuring the new Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 processor and advanced AI capabilities.

## Display
- **Size**: 6.8 inches
- **Type**: Dynamic AMOLED 2X
- **Resolution**: 3120 x 1440 pixels (QHD+, 505 ppi)
- **Refresh Rate**: Adaptive 1Hz to 120Hz
- **Peak Brightness**: 2600 nits
- **Features**:
  - Always-On Display
  - HDR10+ support
  - Corning Gorilla Armor
  - Vision Booster for outdoor visibility
  - Eye Comfort Shield (reduces blue light)

## Processor & Performance
- **Chip**: Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 for Galaxy (4nm process)
- **CPU**: Octa-core (1x3.39GHz Cortex-X4 + 3x3.1GHz Cortex-A720 + 2x2.9GHz Cortex-A720 + 2x2.2GHz Cortex-A520)
- **GPU**: Adreno 750
- **RAM**: 12GB
- **Storage Options**: 256GB, 512GB, 1TB
- **Storage Type**: UFS 4.0

## Key Differentiators

### vs iPhone 15 Pro
**Advantages:**
- Larger display (6.8" vs 6.1")
- Higher resolution (QHD+ vs FHD+)
- Brighter display (2600 nits vs 2000 nits)
- Larger battery (5000mAh vs ~3274mAh)
- Built-in S Pen
- 200MP main camera
- Better zoom capabilities (10x optical quality vs 3x)
- More RAM (12GB vs 8GB)
- Faster charging (45W vs ~20W)
- 7 years of updates
- microSD expansion (on some models)

**Trade-offs:**
- Heavier (232g vs 187g)
- Larger dimensions
- No MagSafe equivalent
- Different ecosystem (Android vs iOS)
