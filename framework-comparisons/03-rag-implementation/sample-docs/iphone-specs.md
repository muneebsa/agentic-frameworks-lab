# iPhone 15 Pro Technical Specifications

## Overview
The iPhone 15 Pro represents Apple's flagship smartphone for 2023, featuring the new A17 Pro chip and titanium design.

## Display
- **Size**: 6.1 inches (iPhone 15 Pro) / 6.7 inches (iPhone 15 Pro Max)
- **Type**: Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion
- **Resolution**: 2556 x 1179 pixels (460 ppi)
- **Refresh Rate**: Adaptive 1Hz to 120Hz
- **Peak Brightness**: 2000 nits (outdoor), 1600 nits (typical max)
- **Features**:
  - Always-On display
  - Dynamic Island
  - HDR support
  - True Tone technology
  - Haptic Touch

## Processor & Performance
- **Chip**: A17 Pro (3nm process)
- **CPU**: 6-core (2 performance + 4 efficiency)
- **GPU**: 6-core
- **Neural Engine**: 16-core
- **RAM**: 8GB
- **Storage Options**: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, 1TB

## Camera System

### Camera Features
- Night mode (all cameras)
- Deep Fusion
- Smart HDR 5
- ProRAW and ProRes video

- 4K video recording at 24fps, 25fps, 30fps, or 60fps

### Front Camera (TrueDepth)
- 12MP (f/1.9 aperture)
- Night mode, Deep Fusion, Smart HDR 5
- 4K video recording at 24fps, 25fps, 30fps, or 60fps

## Battery & Charging

### Battery Life
- **iPhone 15 Pro**: Up to 23 hours video playback
- **iPhone 15 Pro Max**: Up to 29 hours video playback
- Video playback (streamed): Up to 20 hours (Pro) / 25 hours (Pro Max)
- Audio playback: Up to 75 hours (Pro) / 95 hours (Pro Max)

### Charging
- Fast charging: Up to 50% charge in 30 minutes with 20W adapter
- MagSafe wireless charging: Up to 15W
- Qi wireless charging: Up to 7.5W
- USB-C connector (USB 3 speeds up to 10Gb/s)

## Connectivity

### Cellular & Wireless
- 5G (sub-6 GHz and mmWave)
- Gigabit LTE with 4x4 MIMO
- Wi-Fi 6E (802.11ax)
- Bluetooth 5.3
- Ultra Wideband (UWB) chip (second generation)
- NFC with reader mode

### Location
- Precision dual-frequency GPS (GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, QZSS, BeiDou)
- Digital compass
- Wi-Fi positioning
- Cellular positioning
- iBeacon microlocation
